Home Made Chips

Home made, baked potato chips. The perfect snack for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter or Sunflower Oil
  • Salt or other species

Steps

  1. You can start by turning your over in order to pre-heat it. I will recommend 500F or 260C.
  2. The very first step will be to rinse your potatoes.
  3. Once they are clean, you need to peel them and then wash them again.
  4. It's time to slice the potato, they need to be as thin as possible in order to become crisp.
  5. The following step is to arrange them on paper towel to drain their natural moisture
  6. When dry, you can brush both a baking tray with butter and then arrange them.
  7. Depending on your preferences you can also brush the tops of the potatoes.
  8. Bake tha potatoes in center of the over for 15 to 20 minutes or until the sides turn golden brown.
  9. They are ready. You can add salt and other species of your choice.
