Home Made Lemonade

The basic drink that nearly anyone and make.

Ingredients

  • 8 to 10 large lemons
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 quarts water (6 cups)
  • Lemon slices, for serving (optional)

Equipment

  • Measuring cup or medium bowl
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Juicer or citrus reamer, or stand mixer with the paddle attachment
  • Pitcher

Steps

  1. Juice the lemons and strain out the pulp
  2. Combine the lemon juice and sugar, whisk until the sugar is completely dissolved
  3. Add water and chill
  4. Serve with ice and lemon slices
